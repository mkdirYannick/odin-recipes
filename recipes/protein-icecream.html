Low calorie protein Ice-Cream

Description

For me, this recipe is a life saver. I love Ice cream but it is very calorie-dense and sugary. This version is high in protein, and very low in calories for the amount of food you get to eat.

Ingredients

  • 100gr Banana
  • 200gr Ice cubes
  • 100ml Hazelnut milk
  • 40gr Chocolate protein powder
  • 20gr Unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon Xantham gum

Steps

  1. Put all the ingredients in a blender, except the xantham gum.
  2. Mix until the ice cubes are broken down and the preparation is somewhat homogeneous.
  3. Add the xantham gum and mix until the preparation is thick and more voluminous.*
  4. Eat right after when it is still cool, enjoy!

*Be carefull, your blender might struggle and overheat. If that's the case, takes some breaks during this steps.
